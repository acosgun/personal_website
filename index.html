Dr. Akansel Cosgun is a Research Fellow in Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering at Monash University. Prior to joining Monash, he was a Senior Software Engineer at Savioke Inc in California, USA.

Dr. Cosgun's research is at the intersection of Robotics and Machine Learning, with a strong focus on "real problems with real solutions". His research has been applied to a variety of robotic platforms, including self-driving cars, robot arms and mobile service robots.

He received his Ph.D. from in Robotics from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2015. He has published more than 25 contributions across AI and Robotics. He has previously worked at Microsoft, Honda and Toyota.
